# Content Guidelines for Social Media Posting

## General Principles

### 1. Authenticity Over Perfection
- Write in a conversational tone
- Show personality and humanity
- Avoid corporate jargon
- Be genuine and transparent

### 2. Value-First Approach
- Every post should provide value (educate, entertain, inspire)
- Focus on audience needs, not self-promotion
- Share insights, tips, and actionable advice
- Use the 80/20 rule: 80% value, 20% promotion

### 3. Engagement-Driven
- Ask questions to spark conversation
- Encourage comments and shares
- Respond to engagement promptly
- Create content that invites participation

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Facebook

**Character Limit**: 63,206 (but aim for 40-80 words for best engagement)

**Best Practices**:
- Longer posts (100-250 words) can perform well if valuable
- Questions drive 100% more comments
- Native video gets 10x more reach than links
- Post 1-2 times per day maximum

**Content Types That Work**:
- Customer success stories
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Industry insights and tips
- Community-building posts
- Event announcements

**Hashtag Strategy**:
- 1-3 hashtags maximum
- Hashtags less critical than on Instagram
- Use branded hashtags for campaigns

**Timing**:
- Best: 1-4 PM on weekdays
- Avoid: Early mornings and late nights
- Test your specific audience

### Instagram

**Character Limit**: 2,200 (first 125 characters show in feed)

**Best Practices**:
- Visual content is KING - image/video required
- First 125 characters must hook attention
- Hashtags are critical for discovery
- Stories and Reels boost engagement
- Post 1-2 times per day

**Content Types That Work**:
- High-quality images
- Carousel posts (swipe for more)
- Reels (short videos)
- User-generated content
- Inspirational quotes

**Hashtag Strategy**:
- 5-10 hashtags recommended (up to 30 allowed)
- Mix of popular (100K-1M posts) and niche (10K-100K)
- Research hashtags before using
- Create branded hashtag

**Timing**:
- Best: 11 AM - 1 PM on weekdays
- Also good: 7-9 PM
- Avoid: Late night and early morning

### Twitter (X)

**Character Limit**: 280 characters (including hashtags and links)

**Best Practices**:
- Brevity is essential
- Use threads for longer content
- Retweets and replies boost visibility
- Polls drive engagement
- Post 3-5 times per day

**Content Types That Work**:
- Quick tips and insights
- Industry news and commentary
- Threads (numbered tweets)
- Polls and questions
- Timely/trending topics

**Hashtag Strategy**:
- 1-2 hashtags maximum
- More hashtags = less engagement
- Use trending hashtags when relevant
- Create branded hashtag for campaigns

**Timing**:
- Best: 8-10 AM, 6-9 PM
- Weekdays outperform weekends
- Real-time events drive engagement

## Hashtag Best Practices

### Research
- Use platform search to check hashtag popularity
- Analyze competitor hashtags
- Track which hashtags drive engagement
- Update hashtag strategy quarterly

### Selection
- Mix of broad and niche hashtags
- Relevant to content and audience
- Include branded hashtag
- Avoid banned or spam hashtags

### Placement
- **Facebook**: End of post or first comment
- **Instagram**: End of caption or first comment
- **Twitter**: Integrated into text naturally

## Content Calendar

### Posting Frequency
- **Facebook**: 1-2 times per day
- **Instagram**: 1-2 times per day
- **Twitter**: 3-5 times per day

### Content Mix (Weekly)
- 40% Educational (tips, insights, how-tos)
- 30% Engaging (questions, polls, discussions)
- 20% Entertaining (behind-scenes, stories)
- 10% Promotional (products, services)

### Themes by Day
- Monday: Motivation / Week kickoff
- Tuesday: Tips / How-to
- Wednesday: Industry insights
- Thursday: Customer stories
- Friday: Behind-the-scenes / Fun

## Compliance & Safety

### Always Avoid
- False or misleading claims
- Copyrighted content without permission
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Spam or excessive self-promotion
- Sensitive topics without approval

### Always Include
- Proper attribution for quotes/images
- Disclosure for sponsored content (#ad, #sponsored)
- Trigger warnings for sensitive content
- Alt text for images (accessibility)

## Engagement Guidelines

### Responding to Comments
- Respond within 1-2 hours when possible
- Be helpful and friendly
- Address concerns professionally
- Thank users for positive feedback
- Don't engage with trolls

### Handling Negative Feedback
- Acknowledge the concern
- Apologize if appropriate
- Offer to resolve privately (DM)
- Never delete unless spam/abuse
- Learn from constructive criticism

## Performance Metrics

### Track These KPIs
- Engagement rate (likes + comments + shares / followers)
- Reach and impressions
- Click-through rate (for links)
- Follower growth rate
- Best performing content types
- Optimal posting times

### Optimization
- A/B test different content types
- Analyze top-performing posts
- Adjust strategy based on data
- Review metrics weekly
- Update guidelines quarterly

## Content Approval Checklist

Before posting, verify:
- [ ] Content provides value to audience
- [ ] Tone matches brand voice
- [ ] No typos or grammatical errors
- [ ] Hashtags are relevant and researched
- [ ] Character limits respected
- [ ] Call to action is clear
- [ ] Complies with platform policies
- [ ] No sensitive or controversial content
- [ ] Image/video is high quality (if included)
- [ ] Links work and are trackable
- [ ] Scheduled for optimal time
- [ ] Aligns with content calendar
