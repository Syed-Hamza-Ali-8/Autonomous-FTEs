## Content Structure

### Post Format

**Recommended structure**:
```
[Hook/Opening] (1 line)

[Body/Value] (2-3 sentences)

[Call-to-Action] (1 line)

[Hashtags] (3-5 relevant tags)
```

**Character limits**:
- Optimal: 150-300 characters
- Maximum: 3,000 characters
- Hashtags: 3-5 (more looks spammy)
